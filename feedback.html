Send feedback

Email demandgendave@gmail.com with your thoughts, bug reports, feature ideas, or questions.

Please include which app you are using, your device model, iOS version, and any steps that help reproduce the issue.

Email Feedback

Helpful details to include

  • App name: PaddleUp or Pickleball Tracker
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Screenshots if they help explain the issue
  • For PaddleUp: match type, location, and whether it was open or private

Ideas are welcome

If there is a workflow that would help your club, neighborhood, resort, or regular pickleball group, send it over. The goal is to keep the apps simple while solving the everyday friction players actually feel.
