Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a new game?

Tap the "+" icon at the bottom of the screen to start a new game. You can easily add players to both teams, set up the scoring, and begin tracking your match right away. The interface is designed to be quick and intuitive, letting you focus on your game.

How do I add new players?

You can add new players in two ways:

  • From the Players tab, tap the "+" in the top right corner
  • While setting up a new game, tap "ADD TEAMMATE" or "ADD OPPONENT"
Once added, players will be available for all future games.

Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. Make sure to keep your device backed up through iCloud or iTunes to preserve your game history. I'm exploring adding direct iCloud sync in a future update.

How do I view my statistics?

Tap the "Stats" tab to view your comprehensive playing statistics. You can see your total wins, losses, and win percentage. The stats view offers both lifetime statistics and your performance over your last 10 games.

Can I edit or delete a game after it's recorded?

Yes! From the History tab, you can view all your recorded games. If you need to make changes or remove a game, just tap on the game you want to modify.

How do I track games with different scoring rules?

The app is designed to be flexible with scoring. When creating a new game, you can enter any final score that matches your playing format, whether you're playing to 11, 15, or using a different scoring system.
