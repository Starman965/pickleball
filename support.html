Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I add players to my list?
Tap the "Players" tab, then tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Enter the player's name and tap "Save".
Can I edit or delete a game after it's recorded?
Yes! In the History tab, swipe left on any game to reveal the delete option. Note: This action cannot be undone.
How are stats calculated?
Stats are calculated based on your recorded games. Win percentage is calculated by dividing your total wins by total games played.

Apple Watch App

Do I need to have my iPhone nearby to use the Watch app?
The Watch app can function independently, but it needs to sync with your iPhone periodically to update player lists and transfer game data.
How do I sync my Watch app with my iPhone?
The apps sync automatically when your devices are connected. Games recorded on your Watch will transfer to your iPhone when they're in range.
What happens if I record a game when my iPhone is out of range?
Don't worry! The game will be stored on your Watch and automatically sync to your iPhone when the devices reconnect.
Can I add new players from the Watch app?
Player management is handled through the iPhone app. New players added on your iPhone will sync to your Watch automatically.
