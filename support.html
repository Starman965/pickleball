PaddleUp

Finding and managing games

How do I create a match?

Open PaddleUp, choose Play Today or Play Tomorrow, select your time window, choose open or private, and tap Find Players.

What is the difference between open and private?

Open matches are visible to players at the selected location. Private matches are only available to the players or groups you invite.

Can I create singles and doubles matches?

Yes. PaddleUp supports singles and doubles, and match creators can edit the match type when the game details allow it.

Why am I not receiving alerts?

Check notification permissions in iOS Settings and confirm your Open Game Alerts and Private Game Invitations settings in the Profile tab. Alerts for games you join are intended to stay active.

Can I update match details?

Match creators can edit notes and match details. Once a game is ready, players can coordinate and update the official start time and court assignment.

Why do I need to sign in?

Sign-in lets PaddleUp save your profile, match activity, groups, invitations, alerts, and location-specific game information.

Pickleball Tracker

Tracking matches and progress

How do I log a match?

Open Pickleball Tracker, enter the game details, add players, record the score, and save the match to your history.

Does it work with Apple Watch?

Yes. Pickleball Tracker includes Apple Watch support so you can track games more conveniently while you play.

Where is my data stored?

Pickleball Tracker stores match information on your device using Apple platform storage. See the privacy page for more details.

Can I edit a match after saving?

If you notice a mistake, open your match history and edit the saved details where available.

Still need help?

Email demandgendave@gmail.com and include the app name, your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

For PaddleUp support, please also include the location name and whether the issue involved an open match, private match, invite, alert, or profile photo.
