About Me

I discovered pickleball a few years ago and was immediately hooked by its unique blend of strategy, skill, and social interaction. As both a player and developer, I saw an opportunity to create something that would help players like me track their games and progress.

Pickleball Tracker is the result of combining my passion for the sport with my software development skills. It's designed to be the perfect companion for players who want to focus on their game while having a reliable way to track their matches.
