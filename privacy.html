PaddleUp

Information PaddleUp uses

PaddleUp is a social match-making app for pickleball players. To make open games, private invitations, player groups, alerts, and match details work, PaddleUp stores some information in cloud services.

Account and profile information

PaddleUp may use your sign-in method, name, phone number or Apple account identifier, profile photo, selected location, player profile details, and notification settings.

Match and community information

PaddleUp may store games you create or join, match type, notes, invited players or groups, court assignment, start time, player groups you create, and related notifications.

Photos and notifications

If you upload a profile photo, it is stored so other players can recognize you in the app. If you allow notifications, PaddleUp stores a device notification token so it can send game invitations, match updates, court assignments, and start-time changes.

Location context

PaddleUp uses the location or venue you select in the app to show relevant games and players. It is not intended to continuously track your physical location in the background.

Pickleball Tracker

Information Pickleball Tracker uses

Pickleball Tracker is designed to help you log games, scores, partners, opponents, and progress over time.

Match history

The app may store the matches you enter, including scores, dates, players, notes, statistics, achievements, and related history.

Apple Watch support

If you use the Apple Watch app, match-related information may sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch to support the tracking experience.

Local-first design

Pickleball Tracker is focused on personal tracking. Match history is intended for your own use inside the app unless you choose to share information outside the app.

How information is used

Information is used to provide app features, save preferences, show relevant content, support troubleshooting, improve reliability, and communicate with you when you ask for help.

Third-party services

PaddleUp uses Firebase services from Google for authentication, cloud database, storage, notifications, and app infrastructure. Apple services may be used for Sign in with Apple, App Store distribution, iCloud or Watch connectivity, and device-level notification permissions.

Data sharing

Your information is not sold. Information may be visible to other users only when needed for the product experience, such as your name or photo appearing on PaddleUp player lists, private invitations, or match cards.

Your choices

You can adjust notification preferences in the apps and in iOS Settings. You can update profile information where supported. To request help with account or data questions, email demandgendave@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

These apps are intended for general pickleball players and are not directed to children under 13. Please do not use the apps to submit personal information for children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the apps evolve. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.
