Pickleball is addictive—in the best way. The strategy, the tiny adjustments, the community… once you’re in, you’re in. Pickleball Tracker started because we wanted a faster way to log games and a clearer way to understand what was actually helping us improve.

This app is built with care by a pickleball player and shaped by real feedback. If something feels confusing, if you have an idea, or if there’s a stat you wish existed, send it. We read every message and use it to make the app better for everyone.

Thank you for being part of the Pickleball Tracker community.
